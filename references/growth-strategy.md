## Key Principles to Remember

- **The Algorithm Cheat Code:** Every post must end with something that FORCES a reply. A question, a hot take, a "what would you do?" -- the reply count IS the reach.
- **The 30-Minute Golden Window:** When you post, stay online and reply to EVERY comment for the first 30 minutes. This triggers the "Author-Engaged Reply" multiplier -- the highest-weighted signal in the Phoenix algorithm.
- **Vector Consistency:** NEVER post about off-topic subjects. Every single post must reinforce your niche positioning. The algorithm maps your account into a vector space -- pollute it and you become invisible.
- **Don't post and ghost.** A post with 50 likes and zero author replies is algorithmically WORSE than a post with 5 likes and 3 back-and-forth conversations. Engagement is not optional -- it's the product.
